GenZ&Holocaust

Where memory grows

and stories live on.

I am currently designing an interactive concept called the Tree of Remembrance to help Gen Z connect with the story of the children who died in the Holocaust. The project is part of a collaboration with Het Hornemann Huis, a museum started by a man whose grand-nephews, Edo and Lexje, were killed during the war. Since younger generations rarely visit, I am working on a concept of a tree that brings their memory to life. “Cloudy” top that represents dreams and stories never fulfilled and roots that don’t dig into the ground, they rise, representing unfinished growth.
