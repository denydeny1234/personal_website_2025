Escape the Hive

An escape room

that brings us together.

Escape the Hive was a playful escape room experience we built to explore teamwork and creativity. We designed a beehive-shaped box from cardboard and added lights, decorations, and screens to simulate an XR experience. Each of the first three rooms blended our hobbies into fun challenges, while the final room brought it all together, showing how our different interests connect us as a team.
